About this monitor: This tool supports the Review stage of the DPLI DISER Methodology (Diagnose, Investigate, Solve, Execute, Review). The diagnosis of India's state governance challenge and the DPLI State Capacity Framework are presented in the DPLI Flagship Report 2026. This monitor displays the official government data that informs and tracks that analysis.  |  Data transparency: DPLI does not construct, estimate, or modify any scores in this monitor. All figures are reproduced as published by the respective government departments. SDG scores: NITI Aayog SDG India Index 2023-24 (published 12 July 2024). Governance scores: DARPG Good Governance Index 2021 — the most recent edition; GGI 2023 was cancelled by the Government of India; next edition expected December 2025 — scores will be updated upon release. Health data: MoHFW Health Dynamics of India 2022-23. Education data: UDISE+ 2023-24.  |  Coverage: This monitor covers all 28 states and 8 union territories of India where data is available across the four official datasets.  |  Methodology note: DPLI follows established data presentation practice used by the India Justice Report, Public Affairs Index, and similar policy research organisations.  |  Version 3.0, June 2026. Anant Srivastava, Founder, DPLI.
